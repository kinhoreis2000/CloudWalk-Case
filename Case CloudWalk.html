1-Understand the industry

First of all, it is important to define what is an acquire company.

When talking about payment options, we have to deal with some digital issues (If it is an on-line payment). There is where companies like CloudWalk appears. So, it is a good way to define that kind of credit or debit payment environment as a market that specializes in processing payments.

And with that in mind, it gets easier to explain how those companies works.

First, the companies receive the payment information from a costumer (Stores, Parks, supermarket,etc) then they send that information to the bank or other monetary responsible, which will approve the payment method. Futhermore, the acquirer is responsible to send the monetary value to the costumer.

1.1-Difference between acquirer, sub-acquirer and payment gateway

While Acquire companies are really independent when talking about the payment money flow, the sub–acquire companies deal with other integration problems, which can make that kind of business more unsustainable to huge companies, in detriment of those issues, sub–acquire needs to get higher rates charging each transaction. Nonetheless, to small stores the low implementation cost gets more attractable.

A payment gateway, on the other hand, is a mechanism that sends information about purchases made in your store at the point of sale. They are responsible for consolidating most of the transactions and organizing the money flow to the main players of the acquire market.

1.2-How the supply chain fits into the ecosystem of means of payments

The supply chain, or Supply Chain, is the set of methods and processes that involve the flow of goods from the purchase of raw materials to the final consumer. The activities included within this process include the purchase of raw materials, packaging, product manufacturing, storage, transportation, distribution, sale and customer service. It is also noticed that there is a relationship between the theme and the industry.

It is easy to see that this network acts directly in the areas of customer, reseller, and supplier. To facilitate the use of credit among stakeholders, companies such as CloudWalk help large companies to buy products from suppliers, while sub-acquire helps small, medium, and large entrepreneurs to buy from retailers. It is a fully connected, complex, and objective network, which without the presence of different payment methods would not be possible.

1.3-How the agile delivery of the card machine would benefit everyone involved.

With the agility in obtaining payment machines, it is possible to obtain more objective and accurate results in the supply chain. Even causing an optimization of time and, consequently, of the money involved. The more customers have different forms of payment and the flow of money is easily drained, this certainly impacts from the purchase of raw materials to the material developed for the customer.
